SafePath Case Study

MMCM-DVO // 6.07° N

SafePath is a community-driven travel safety application developed to address the real-world challenges travellers face navigating unfamiliar regions. By integrating real-time crowdsourced safety evaluations with intelligent route planners and active safety tracking, it ensures safety isn’t a afterthought, but an integral part of the itinerary.

01 / AUTHWelcome & Setup

A minimalist onboarding workflow focusing on quick credentials validation, layout personalization, and crucial permission requests like geo-location access.

02 / NAVIGATIONReal-time Map Routing

Interactive vector maps featuring visual color gradients reflecting route safety ratings, crowdsourced reviews, and safety hazard indicators.

03 / EXPLORELocation Insight Panels

Detailed location panels presenting user ratings, local reports, and travel suggestions categorized by daylight and nighttime safety factors.

04 / PLANNERItinerary Planner

A cohesive calendar workspace to map trip routes, list itinerary stops, and review coordinates, ensuring travels stay within safe operational boundaries.

05 / FEEDBACKUser Feedback Reports

Crowdsourced reporting portals allowing users to pin active feedback, describe regional warnings, and rate safety criteria to support fellow travelers.

HCI Design Blueprint

Designing high-impact tools requires rigorous research, continuous ideation, and user validation. For SafePath, my team followed a structured, human-centered design loop to ensure every feature answered a real traveler need.

Personal Reflection

Winning Best Tourism & Service Application at CCIS Innovision 2026 was a defining moment in my academic journey. Developing SafePath from a concept into a fully functional application taught me the full HCI lifecycle from user research and wireframing to usability testing and iterative prototyping. Presenting our project to the panel pushed me to articulate our design decisions clearly and defend the user-centered choices we made. This award validated the countless hours our team spent refining the interface, integrating safety features like emergency contacts and crowdsourced reviews, and ensuring the app genuinely served traveller needs. Beyond the technical skills, I learned the value of collaboration, constructive feedback, and resilience especially when things didn't work the first time. This experience reinforced my passion for building products that make a real difference in people's everyday lives.

Memorable day! 🌟
